A simple, clean body measurement tracking app

Exercise and diet are the keys to attaining a healthy body, but physical changes can be difficult to notice as they are slow and gradual. This can lead to a loss of motivation to continue. BodiTraq helps by allowing you to record your body measurements so that these incremental changes are visible. Even when you don't feel like you're making progress, BodiTraq will prove that your waist is shrinking, your arms growing, and your overall body transforming.
